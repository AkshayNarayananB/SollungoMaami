# 🥥 Coconut Barfi | Classic Indian Coconut Sweet

Just 3 ingredients — melt-in-mouth coconut barfi!

**Coconut Barfi** is a classic Indian sweet made with fresh grated coconut, sugar, and a touch of cardamom. Soft, chewy, and full of flavor, this quick dessert is a festival favorite — especially during **Diwali**, **Navratri**, and **Krishna Jayanthi**. It’s easy to make and melts in your mouth with every bite!

---

## ✨ Ingredients

# 🥥 Grated coconut (fresh or desiccated) – 1 cup  
# 🍬 Sugar – 1½ cups  
# 🌿 Cardamom powder – ¼ tsp  
# 🧈 Ghee – 2 to 3 tbsp  
# 🥜 Roasted cashews (chopped) – 2 to 3 tbsp  

---

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions

### Preparation  
# 1️⃣ Grease a plate or tray with ghee and set aside.  
# 2️⃣ Chop roasted cashews into small pieces.  
# 3️⃣ Keep all ingredients ready as the process moves quickly.  

### Making the Sugar Syrup  
# 4️⃣ In a heavy-bottomed pan, add sugar and cook on medium heat.  
# 5️⃣ Stir continuously until sugar melts and forms a thick syrup (soft ball stage).  

### Adding Coconut  
# 6️⃣ Add grated coconut to hot sugar syrup.  
# 7️⃣ Reduce heat to medium-low.  
# 8️⃣ Stir continuously and cook for 2-3 minutes until boiling.  
# 9️⃣ Continue stirring as mixture thickens.  

### Flavoring and Final Cooking  
# 🔟 Add cardamom powder and mix well.  
# 1️⃣1️⃣ Increase heat to medium and stir for 5 minutes until mixture thickens and leaves sides of pan.  
# 1️⃣2️⃣ Add roasted cashews and mix well.  

### Setting the Barfi  
# 1️⃣3️⃣ Keep stirring continuously as mixture can splutter.  
# 1️⃣4️⃣ When mixture thickens and leaves pan sides, turn off heat.  
# 1️⃣5️⃣ Immediately transfer to greased plate.  
# 1️⃣6️⃣ Level quickly with ladle/spatula and press gently to even out.  

### Cutting  
# 1️⃣7️⃣ Cut while warm using a sharp knife for neat shapes.  
# 1️⃣8️⃣ Cutting cold barfi may cause breakage.  

---

## ✅ Tips  

# ✔️ Continuous stirring is crucial throughout the process.  
# ✔️ Have all ingredients ready before starting.  
# ✔️ Work quickly once mixture is ready.  
# ✔️ Perfect for festivals and celebrations.  
# ✔️ Great way to use fresh coconut.  

---

## 🍽️ Serving  

# 🍽️ Serve at room temperature.  
# 🛍️ Store in an airtight container for up to one week.  

---

This traditional coconut barfi is especially popular during festivals in the month of **Aadi**, when coconut is commonly used. It’s a simple recipe with few ingredients that anyone can easily try!
