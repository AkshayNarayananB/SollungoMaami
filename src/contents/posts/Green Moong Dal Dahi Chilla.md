## ðŸ‘©â€ðŸ³ Method

### **Grind Batter**
1. Soak moong dal for 1 hour.  
2. Rinse well & grind with chilies, ginger, coriander & little water.  
3. Keep texture slightly coarse.  

### **Prepare Mix**
4. Add onion, capsicum & carrot.  
5. Mix in besan, masalas, lemon juice & hing.  
6. Add **salt only before cooking** and mix.

### **Cook Chilla**
7. Heat tawa & grease lightly.  
8. Shape batter into small thick rounds (like mini uttapam).  
9. Cook on **medium-low flame** till golden on both sides.

### **Make Mint Curd Chutney**
10. Grind all chutney ingredients smooth.  
11. Whisk curd with chaat masala & mix with chutney.

### **Assemble**
12. Place hot chillas on plate.  
13. Spoon curd generously.  
14. Drizzle mint chutney & serve immediately.
