##  Ingredients

-  1 cup urad dal  
-  Salt to taste  
-  A pinch of asafoetida (hing)  
-  1-inch piece ginger, finely chopped  
-  2-3 green chillies, finely chopped  
-  Few curry leaves, finely chopped  
-  Water as needed for grinding  
-  Oil for deep frying
