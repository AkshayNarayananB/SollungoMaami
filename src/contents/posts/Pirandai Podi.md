### Step 1: Prep Pirandai

### Step 2: Roast Ingredients  
1. Dry roast **chana dal** on medium flame until light golden & aromatic.  
2. Roast **coriander seeds** until crisp and they give a puffing sound.

5. Finally roast **fenugreek seeds** on low flame till golden.
